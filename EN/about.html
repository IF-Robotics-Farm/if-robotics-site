CEO Message

Dear valued customers, Founded in 2025 with the vision of building “robots that can handle every smart-farm task,” IF Robotics has focused on four core line-ups—high-altitude lift, transport robot, spray robot and harvest robot—to boost growers’ productivity. First, Technology Innovation — By 2027 we will realise a fully domestic robot platform using 3D Depth-RGB fusion vision and a mecanum-drive chassis. Second, Product Road-map — We will successively launch the L100 in 2025, M100 in 2026, S100 in 2027 and H100 in 2028, setting the standard for smart-farm automation. Third, Affordable Pricing Strategy — Through RaaS and SaaS models, farmers can enjoy innovation without heavy upfront investment. Fourth, Global Challenge — Starting with our first export in 2028, we aim to lead the worldwide agricultural-robot market. Thank you for your continued support.

Hun-Joong Lee, CEO, IF Robotics Co., Ltd.
